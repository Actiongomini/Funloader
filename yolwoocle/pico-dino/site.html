Pico Dino

Pico Dino is a reimagination of Google Chrome's offline dinosaur game!

Features a new moveset and graphics!

Controls:

  • Jump - Up arrow / C
  • Crouch - Down arrow / X

Made with love using Pico-8 by Yolwoocle
Jan 2025 update : 60 FPS port courtesy of JPshoe
